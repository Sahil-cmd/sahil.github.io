Hello, I'm Sahil.


I am a Research Engineer at Agency for Science, Technology and Research (A*STAR) with the ROS-Industrial group, where I work in the domain of autonomous robotics.

Previously, I worked as a researcher at Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD), a public university in Singapore, with the Singapore-MIT Alliance for Research & Technology Centre (SMART) and the SUTD-MIT International Design Centre (IDC) in the autonomous driving and additive manufacturing domains.
